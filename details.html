ORANGE CAKE

The Components

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour.
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon orange zest
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Prepartion Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a cake pan with a thin layer of butter and dust it with flour.
  2. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and sugar until you have a light and homogeneous mixture.
  3. Add the orange juice and vegetable oil, and mix well.
  4. Add the flour, baking powder, salt, and orange zest. Stir until the ingredients are well combined and a smooth batter is formed.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan, filling it about two-thirds full.
  6. Place the cake pan in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 35-40 minutes, or until the cake is golden and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cake to cool completely in the pan, then remove it from the pan and transfer it to a cooling rack.
  8. You can decorate the orange cake with powdered sugar or prepare an orange glaze by mixing orange juice with powdered sugar and drizzling it over the cake.
  9. Serve the orange cake and enjoy!
